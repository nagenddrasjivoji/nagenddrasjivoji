Services

Explore our diverse range of healing modalities tailored to meet your unique needs.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture, rooted in traditional Chinese medicine, involves inserting fine needles at precise points on the body to balance energy (Qi) flow. It helps alleviate pain, reduce stress, and treat various health conditions.

Acutouch

Acutouch blends acupressure and energy healing to unblock and harmonize the flow of energy (Qi), promoting relaxation and well-being, especially for emotional and physical concerns.

Hypnotherapy

Hypnotherapy uses hypnosis to help clients access their subconscious, addressing issues like anxiety, smoking cessation, weight loss, phobias, and more, facilitating personal growth and healing.

Regression Therapy

Regression therapy helps clients recall and relive past events, resolving emotional, mental, or physical blockages and facilitating healing from trauma, anxiety, and self-sabotaging behaviors.

Numerology

Numerology explores the influence of numbers based on your birth date and name. It offers insights into your strengths, challenges, and life purpose, guiding decision-making in relationships and career.

Vastu

Vastu Shastra balances the five elements within a space, creating environments conducive to health, happiness, and success by harmonizing energy flow for mental clarity and well-being.
